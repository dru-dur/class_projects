First Ford Truck

The Model TT truck launches in July 1917, nine years after the Model T. The model TT offers bed configurations just like the Model T did. The Ford Model T inspired countless aftermarket accessories. Truck conversion kits from the Smith Form-a-Truck Company allowed a Model T owner to extend and reinforce the car's frame, and to connect the rear axle and rear wheels with a chain drive. The kits also included heavier rear wheels with solid tires to replace the T's pneumatic tires.
